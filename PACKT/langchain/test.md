# Document about Famous Composers

## Johann Sebastian Bach
Johann Sebastian Bach was born on March 21, 1685 in Eisenach, Germany. He was one of the greatest Baroque composers and is known for his mathematical precision and complex compositions. His works include the Brandenburg Concertos, The Art of Fugue, and the Goldberg Variations.

## Wolfgang Amadeus Mozart
Wolfgang Amadeus Mozart was born on January 27, 1756 in Salzburg, Austria. He was a prolific composer of the Classical period and composed over 600 works. His most famous compositions include Don Giovanni, The Magic Flute, and Symphony No. 40 in G minor.

## Ludwig van Beethoven
Ludwig van Beethoven was born on December 17, 1770 in Bonn, Germany. He was one of the most influential composers of all time, bridging the Classical and Romantic periods. His symphonies, particularly the Ninth Symphony with its "Ode to Joy," remain some of the most celebrated works in music.

## General Information
These composers shaped the course of Western classical music and their influence extends across centuries. Their compositions continue to be performed, studied, and celebrated worldwide.
