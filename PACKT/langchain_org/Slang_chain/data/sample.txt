Sarvam AI Platform Guide

Sarvam AI is an advanced artificial intelligence platform designed to provide enterprise-grade language models and AI solutions. The platform is built with a focus on accessibility, affordability, and effectiveness.

Key Features:
1. Multilingual Support: Sarvam AI supports multiple languages including Hindi, Tamil, Telugu, Kannada, and English.
2. Custom Models: Users can train and deploy custom models tailored to their specific needs.
3. API Integration: Easy-to-use APIs for seamless integration with existing systems.
4. Real-time Processing: Fast and efficient processing of large datasets.

Sarvam AI Models:
- Sarvam-7b: A 7 billion parameter model optimized for efficiency and speed.
- Sarvam-m: A medium-sized model balancing performance and resource requirements.
- Sarvam-l: A large model for compute-intensive tasks requiring maximum accuracy.

The platform is designed to democratize access to advanced AI technology, making it available to organizations of all sizes. Whether you're building a chatbot, content generation system, or advanced analytics platform, Sarvam AI provides the tools and infrastructure needed for success.
